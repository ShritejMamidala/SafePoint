How to Use SafePoint

  1. Explore the Map: At the top of the main page, you’ll see an interactive map. This map displays pins for publicly available evacuation centers.
  2. View Evacuation Centers: Below the map is a list of evacuation centers, sorted based on their distance from your current location.
  3. Get Details: Click on a map pin or a list item to view more details about that location, such as name, address, and capacity (if available).
  4. Dark/Light Mode: Use the button in the top right corner to toggle between dark and light themes.

Note:

SafePoint is designed to help users locate the nearest evacuation centers using real-time, publicly available data. The map and list will update based on your location.
